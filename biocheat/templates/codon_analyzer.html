Codon Analyzer

It will translate your codon sequence and display codon ratio graph and codon adaptation index. You can get affinity between your codon sequence and selected organism.

How to use

Search organism to compare. You need to input least 3 characters to search organism in our database (datasets are from ftp.kazusa.or.jp/pub/codon/current/). Select translation table. You can change values clicking show button.

Input your base sequence. Characters except A, G, T, U, C will be ignored (not case sensitive). All T will be replace to U.

In translation result, codon encodes terminate will be shown as 'Ter'.

In graph, steelblue bars are data from codon sequence you entered and black bars are data from organism you selected.
